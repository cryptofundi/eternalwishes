Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

Eternal Wishes respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is involved when you use our platform and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

When you use Eternal Wishes, the following information may be involved:

  • Wallet Address — your public wallet address is used to interact with the blockchain when you connect your wallet.
  • Message Content — the wishes you create are stored permanently on the blockchain and are publicly accessible.
  • Media Files — if you upload images or audio, they are stored on decentralized storage networks (IPFS).
  • Basic Technical Data — standard browser information may be processed to ensure the website functions correctly. We do not use this for tracking or profiling.

2. How We Use Information

We use the above information only to:

  • Enable you to create and store wishes on the blockchain
  • Display your wishes and moments within the platform
  • Maintain and improve the functionality of the website

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Blockchain Data

Eternal Wishes uses blockchain technology. Data written to the blockchain is public, permanent, and cannot be deleted or modified. Your wallet address and message content will be visible to anyone. Please review your content carefully before confirming a transaction.

4. Decentralized Storage (IPFS)

If you upload media, files may be stored on decentralized networks such as IPFS. While designed for long-term availability, we do not guarantee permanent availability of media files. You are responsible for any content you upload.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or user profiling. We may use minimal technical processes necessary for the website to function correctly.

6. Third-Party Services

Your interaction with Eternal Wishes may involve third-party services including crypto wallets (e.g., MetaMask), blockchain networks (e.g., Base), and IPFS storage providers. These services operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We do not store personal user data on centralized servers. Blockchain transactions are irreversible. You are responsible for securing your wallet and private keys.

8. Children's Privacy

Eternal Wishes is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may reach us via our community channels.

Summary

  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking or selling of data
  • Messages are public and permanent
  • Media stored on decentralized networks
  • You remain in control of your wallet and content

Eternal Wishes is designed to be simple, transparent, and respectful of your privacy.
